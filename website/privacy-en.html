Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 30, 2026

Shanghai SurpriseMan Technology Co., LTD. ("we") developed FriesOnThePier, a free open-source AI coding assistant plugin. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. What the plugin does

FriesOnThePier is a plugin that runs within AI coding tools like Codex / Claude Code. It runs locally in your toolchain and does not require account registration. Its core function is to add a reminder at the end of AI responses during meal times, and upon your agreement, assist in placing an order via McDonald's official MCP service.

2. Information we collect

The plugin itself does not collect any personal data:

  • Does not transmit any data to our servers.
  • Does not require account registration.
  • All configurations (including MCP Token) are stored only locally on your device in your Codex / Claude Code user configuration files.
  • Does not log, upload, or store any text or actions you input during sessions.

3. McDonald's MCP Token

If you choose to use the ordering feature, you need to provide the McDonald's China official MCP Token. This Token:

  • Is stored only in the local user-level config or environment variables of Codex / Claude Code on your device.
  • Is used only to make requests directly to the McDonald's MCP service on your behalf.
  • Will not be transmitted to or stored on our servers.

The McDonald's MCP service is operated by McDonald's China. Please refer to McDonald's China's privacy policy for their data processing rules.

4. Open Source Transparency

FriesOnThePier is completely open-source under the MIT License. You can view all the code at github.com/DreamArc77/FriesOnThePier. There are no hidden data collection mechanisms.

5. Cookies & Analytics

This official website does not use advertising cookies, third-party analytics tools, or tracking pixels.

6. Your Rights

Since the plugin does not collect personal data, we do not hold any personal information that can be accessed or deleted. If you have any questions, please contact us using the information below, and we will reply within 30 days.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with the new effective date.
